"We need to convince the auto manufacturers to come up with a vehicle that's harder to steal.  I'm not an engineer but I find it hard to believe that we can't develop a car that can't be stolen by a twelve year old car thief in under a minute.  I think we need to develop minimum standards for theft deterrents to new automobiles."

Statement before COMMITTEE ON THE JUNDICIARY
by Richard Jeffares Chairman of the Anti-Theft Committee New York Dec. 9, 1991
